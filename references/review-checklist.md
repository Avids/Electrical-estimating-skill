# Review checklist

## Electrical distribution
- Verify one-line, plans, schedules, fault ratings, feeder sizes, grounding, metering, controls, and equipment ratings agree.
- Check equipment delivery path, housekeeping pads, clearances, ventilation, seismic restraint, shutdowns, temporary power, testing, and startup.
- Match each load to a source and each feeder to a destination.

## Lighting and controls
- Cross-check lighting plans, RCPs, fixture schedules, control diagrams, emergency designations, circuiting, mounting, dimming, sensors, relays, and interfaces.
- Check ceiling type, finish, support, access, aiming, architectural integration, and mock-up requirements.

## Fire alarm
- Cross-check floor plans, riser, sequence of operation, matrix, specifications, device schedules, annunciation, monitoring, interfaces, verification, and programming.
- Separate installation, supply, programming, verification, and monitoring responsibilities.

## Communications and IT
- Cross-check rack elevations, room layouts, outlet plans, risers, cable schedules, fibre schedules, pathways, grounding, UPS/PDU, patching, active equipment, testing, and labelling.
- Confirm outlet locations before rough-in where conduit is by electrical.

## Security
- Cross-check device plans, door hardware, access-control diagrams, intrusion, CCTV, intercom, power supplies, network requirements, head-end equipment, conduit, cabling, programming, and commissioning.
- Coordinate doors, frames, electrified hardware, power-transfer devices, and lock power.

## BAS and controls interfaces
- Identify every BAS point or data outlet requiring electrical rough-in, power, conduit, or network connection.
- Confirm exact device/equipment location, mounting height, conduit size, termination point, and responsible party before rough-in.

## Architectural and structural
- Check wall types, fire ratings, finishes, ceilings, elevations, millwork, doors, waterproofing, equipment-room layouts, supports, sleeves, embeds, coring limits, and structural backing.

## Mechanical coordination
- Check final equipment locations, disconnects, starters/VFDs, voltage/phase, MCA/MOCP, controls, heat tracing, smoke control, dampers, valves, condensate, clearances, and packaged-equipment responsibilities.
